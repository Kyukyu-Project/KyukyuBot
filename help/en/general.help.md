`?help`

*Get information on using bot commands.*

**Aliases**
*  `?h`
*  `?command`
*  `?cmd`

**Usages**
* `?help [--general]`
* `?help <--admin|--owner>`
* `?help <command-name>`

**Options**
* `-g, --general       `List all general commands
* `-a, --admin         `List all guild administrator commands
* `-o, --owner, --dev  `List all bot owner commands

**Examples**
* `?help --general`
* `?help ping`
